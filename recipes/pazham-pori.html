Pazham Pori (Banana Fritters)

Description

Sweet banana slices coated in a light flour batter and deep-fried to golden perfection. A popular tea-time snack in Kerala.

Ingredients:

  1. Ripe bananas (Nendran variety)
  2. all-purpose flour
  3. sugar
  4. Turmeric
  5. Water
  6. Oil for frying

Steps:

  • Peel and slice the bananas lengthwise.
  • Make a thick batter with flour, sugar, turmeric, and water.
  • Dip the banana slices in the batter.
  • Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.
  • Serve hot.
