Unniyappam

Small, sweet, and fluffy fritters made from rice flour, jaggery, and ripe bananas, fried in ghee or oil. A festive snack loved for its soft texture and rich flavor.

Ingredients:

  1. Rice flour
  2. jaggery
  3. ripe bananas
  4. coconut pieces
  5. sesame seeds
  6. oil

Steps:

  • Make a batter using mashed bananas, melted jaggery, rice flour, coconut pieces, and sesame seeds.
  • Heat an appe pan, pour ghee or oil in the molds.
  • Dip the banana slices in the batter.
  • Drop spoonfuls of batter into the molds and fry until golden brown on both sides.
  • Serve warm.
