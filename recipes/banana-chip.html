Banana Chips (Kaya Upperi)

Description

Crispy, deep-fried slices of Nendran bananas cooked in coconut oil, seasoned lightly with salt. A crunchy snack often enjoyed during festivals.

Ingredients:

  1. Raw bananas (Nendran variety)
  2. Salt
  3. Turmeric

Steps:

  • Peel and thinly slice raw bananas.
  • Soak the slices in water mixed with turmeric and salt for 10 minutes.
  • Heat coconut oil in a deep pan.
  • Fry the slices until crisp and golden.
  • Drain excess oil on paper towels.
